About

We are a K-8 Catholic elementary school of 130 students located at 370 Kingston Mills Rd. in Kingston, Ontario. We offer before and after-school care on-site, and we are also home to an Ontario Learning Years Centre for families with preschool aged children.

Director of education - David DeSaints

Achievements

First Math Competiton

In June, 13 math teams competed in the first ever ALCDSB Grade 5/6 math tournament in honour of St. Marguerite Bourgeoys’ 400th anniversary. Sr. Rosemary and Sr. Mildred of the Congregation of the Sisters of Notre Dame awarded the medals and generously donated a trophy in honour of St. Marguerite Bourgeoys. Congratulations to all teams who competed!

Discipleship

Each year during Catholic Education Week, ALCDSB recognizes the exemplary accomplishments of students, staff and community partners who consistently demonstrate outstanding service. The recipients of this year’s awards were celebrated at the Catholic Education Week board wide Mass at St. Joseph Catholic Church in Belleville. Congratulations to this year’s recipients!

What's new

  • Remote Learning

    As announced by Premier Ford today (Dec. 21, 2020) the province will enter a period of Public Health lockdown restrictions beginning at 12:01 a.m., December 26, 2020. We confirm that ALCDSB elementary school students will participate in remote learning from January 4-January 8, 2021. ALCDSB secondary students will participate in remote learning from January 4 – 25, 2021. More information will be shared as we receive it. Please stay tuned to the Board's website and social media channels for further updates.

  • Terry Fox milestone

    The staff of Our Lady of Mount Carmel wish to thank the students and their families for their involvement and generosity with the Terry Fox Fundraiser. During these unprecedented times, we knew that the fundraiser would look different this year and that we would not be able to collect funds like in years past. OLMC set a small goal of $4589.50 in hopes that we would collect enough money to reach a milestone of $100,000. We are pleased to announce that once again, the OLMC community has answered the call to help those most vulnerable and together this year we raised $5765.00. This means that over the span of 19 runs, OLMC is now responsible for having fundraised $101, 175.41 for the Terry Fox Foundation. We are extremely proud of our school community! Amazing work cougars!
